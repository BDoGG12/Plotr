Overview

The short version

Plotr does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any server. Everything you create in the app — posts, ideas, checklists, footage attachments — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and is included in your standard iCloud Backup.

This Privacy Policy explains how Plotr ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use the Plotr iOS application. By using Plotr, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Section 1

Information we don't collect

Plotr is designed to work entirely offline. We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Your content — posts, ideas, scripts, checklists, or footage
  • Your location or device location data
  • Browsing history or app usage patterns sent to our servers
  • Device identifiers used for advertising
  • Contacts, camera roll, or microphone data beyond what you explicitly choose to attach

No account is required to use Plotr. We have no way to identify you as an individual user.

Section 2

Information we do collect

We collect minimal, anonymous information to understand how the app is performing:

  • Crash reports — if the app crashes, Apple may send us an anonymised crash log through App Store Connect. These logs contain no personal data and are used solely to fix bugs.
  • Waitlist email — if you signed up for the waitlist via our website, we store your email address to contact you about launch updates. You can unsubscribe at any time.

That's it. We don't use analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, or any third-party data collection tools inside the app.

Section 3

How your data is stored

All content you create in Plotr is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This includes:

  • Posts and their metadata (title, stage, platform, due date, checklist)
  • Ideas in your Idea Vault
  • Video footage attachments (stored as secure bookmark references to your Photos library)

This data is automatically included in your iPhone backup if you have iCloud Backup or iTunes Backup enabled. Backups are managed entirely by Apple and are subject to Apple's privacy policy.

We have no access to your device backups or the data stored within Plotr.

Section 4

Photos & video access

When you attach footage to a post, Plotr requests access to your Photos library. This permission is used exclusively to let you select video clips to attach to your posts.

  • We do not upload your photos or videos to any server
  • We do not scan, analyse, or index your Photos library
  • Video files are referenced locally on your device only
  • You can revoke Photos access at any time in Settings → Privacy → Photos

Section 5

Third parties & sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third party for any purpose.

Plotr does not integrate with any advertising networks, analytics platforms, or data brokers. The only third-party service we use is Apple's App Store infrastructure for app distribution and crash reporting.

If we are ever required by law to disclose information, we will comply with applicable legal requirements. However, since we hold no personal data about our users, there is effectively nothing to disclose.

Section 6

Deleting your data

You are in full control of your data at all times.

  • To delete all Plotr data from your device, simply delete the app from your iPhone
  • To remove your email from our waitlist, email us at support@plotrapp.com and we will remove it immediately
  • Individual posts and ideas can be deleted inside the app at any time

Because no data is stored on our servers, there is no account deletion process required.

Section 7

Children's privacy

Plotr is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@plotrapp.com and we will delete it promptly.

Section 8

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify users through the App Store update notes.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of Plotr after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Section 9

Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how Plotr handles your information, please reach out:

  • Email: support@plotrapp.com
  • Support page: plotrapp.com/support

We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 48 hours.
